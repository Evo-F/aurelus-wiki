**The Party** refers to the group of adventurers who are at the center of [[The Great Aurelusan Conflict]].

## Party Members
The Party consists of the following members:
- Marius.
- Jim Irish.
- Ulfgar Fireforge.
- Eirros Yestumal.
- Max the Blink-Dog.
